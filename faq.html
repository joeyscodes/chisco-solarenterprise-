Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about going solar — answered honestly by Lagos' trusted installers.

Costs vary depending on your energy needs. A small home system can start from around ₦500,000, while larger commercial setups may reach ₦5 million or more. We provide free assessments and custom quotes tailored to your budget.

Quality solar panels last 25–30 years with minimal degradation. Lithium batteries typically last 8–15 years depending on usage. We only install Tier‑1 equipment with solid warranties.

Batteries are optional but highly recommended in Nigeria due to grid instability. They store excess solar energy for night use and keep your power on during grid failures.

Most residential installations are completed within 3–7 days. Larger commercial projects may take 2–4 weeks. We work efficiently without compromising quality.

We provide manufacturer warranties: typically 10–12 years on inverters, 25 years on panels, and 5–10 years on batteries. Additionally, we give a 1‑year workmanship warranty on all installations.
