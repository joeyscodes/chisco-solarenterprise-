About Chisco Solar

Our Story

Powering Nigeria, One Panel at a Time

Founded in Lagos, Chisco Solar Enterprises was born from a simple belief: every Nigerian deserves reliable, affordable electricity. We saw businesses shut down due to generator failures, families paying fortunes for fuel, and communities left in the dark — and we decided to change that.

Today, we are a trusted solar installation company serving homes, offices, schools, churches, and businesses across Lagos. Our certified team has completed over 200 installations, delivering millions of naira in energy savings to our clients.

🎯 Our Mission

To make reliable solar power accessible and affordable to every Nigerian home and business, reducing dependence on generators and the national grid.

🔭 Our Vision

To become Nigeria's most trusted solar energy company, leading the transition to clean, sustainable power across West Africa.
