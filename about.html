About Chisco Solar Enterprise

Powering Nigeria's Future

Founded with a clear purpose to solve Nigeria's energy crisis, Chisco Solar Enterprise has grown from a small local installer to one of Lagos's most trusted solar energy providers.

We understand the unique power challenges faced by homes and businesses in Nigeria. Erratic grid supply and expensive generator maintenance shouldn't hold you back. That's why we focus on delivering robust, high-quality solar and inverter systems that guarantee 24/7 power availability.

Our commitment to using premium tier-1 equipment combined with expert engineering ensures that every system we deploy stands the test of time, providing our clients with peace of mind and significant long-term savings.

Our Mission

"To provide affordable, reliable, and sustainable solar energy solutions to every home and business in Nigeria, empowering our clients to take control of their energy future."

Our Vision

"To be Nigeria's most trusted and innovative solar energy company, leading the transition towards a cleaner, brighter, and more sustainable energy ecosystem."
